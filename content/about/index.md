The Digital Pensieve is not merely a tool for recollection; it’s a portal to the past and a
window into the present. It is a place where memories can be examined and understood.

I’m Erick, a technology architect, lifelong reader, and naturally curious collector of ideas.
This site is where I write about the subjects that hold my attention—history, books and
audiobooks, technology, travel, gardening, food, and the occasional project or experience
worth preserving.

The name Digital Pensieve reflects what I want this site to be: a personal archive of
thoughts, discoveries, reviews, and reflections. Some posts explore the past through
military history and historical fiction. Others document places I have visited, things I
have learned, meals I have made, or projects I have worked through. The topics may vary,
but they are connected by curiosity and the desire to capture ideas before they disappear.
