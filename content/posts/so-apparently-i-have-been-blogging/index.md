So apparently, I have been blogging since 2010 per my profile on this
site… I know I have never posted anything on any blog site, but I guess,
I must have logged into blogger once, long ago.   While I have been
thinking about blogging off and on for a while, I actually have never
posted anything.   I wanted to create a separate place to post things I
didn’t want to share specifically on other social media sites, or where
I can go more in depth than I normally do on those sites.   I am not
going to post anything from the past, just new things going forward.  So
finally, after 14 years of procrastination here is my first post.
